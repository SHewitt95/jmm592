The data portrays the percentage of 18 to 24-year-olds per race/ethnicity who attend 2 or 4-year colleges. For example, of all "White, non-Hispanic" 18 to 24-year-olds, 42.1 percent went to a 2 or 4-year college in 2012.

One statistic that stands out is the change in Hispanic college attendance between 2000 and 2012. The rate nearly doubles over the 12-year span.
